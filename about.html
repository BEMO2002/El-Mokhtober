About Us

About Al Mokhtabar Lab

Al Mokhtabar is the largest private medical laboratory testing company in the Middle East, providing diagnostic laboratory services.

Read More

Our Vision

To be the recognized leader in the field of laboratory medicine in the Middle East. This vision is achieved through:

The commitment of our leaders and employees to our mission and core values.

The implementation of optimized business processes.

The innovative application of new technologies.

Our Mission

To provide quality laboratory diagnostic information to help improve the health and well-being of the communities we serve. This mission is achieved through:

Providing high quality medical laboratory services using the most advanced technology.

Placing the needs of each individual patient first.

Emphasizing on courtesy, compassion and professional expertise.

Striving for accuracy of testing, responsiveness to our clients' needs and promptness.

Acquiring, disseminating, and applying knowledge through the innovative use of technology.

Creating/expanding mutually cooperative partnerships.
